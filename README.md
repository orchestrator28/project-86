# project-86
Helping restaurant owners 86 the stress of compliance.
## The problem ##
Restaurant owners are already stretched thin, putting out fires and dealing with day to day operations. Most of them cannot afford to hire someone to keep track of all the obligations and compliance, they have bills to be paid, license renewals, fire inspections,health department inspections, tax filings and a million other deadlines, to remember, plan for, and prepare for. Accountants and bookeepers do not typically handle this and if they do it costs to much $$$
Missing one of these obligations can result in fines, additional time spent fixing it or even paying someone else to fix it. (even worse SAD FACE)
## The Vision ##
Project 86 is an Ai powered platform that accompanies and reminds business owners of these obligations, which a single photo upload or entry, we log, investigate and remind you of an obligation with plenty of time for you to get that taken care of.
Our goal is simple: **Restaurant owners should never miss a deadline because they were too busy dealing with day to day work, Project 86 will log and remember for them**
### Version 1 MVP ###
The first version will allow restaurant owners to:
- Track recurring obligations
- Store related documents
- Receive reminders before deadlines
- Organize obligations by category
- Upload documents for AI-assisted extraction of renewal dates
## Why am I building this ##
1. Build a project that helps restaurant owners like me
2. Learn how to build something with the exisiting tools in the market atm
3. Every major decision, feature, and lesson learned will be documented throughout the project.
