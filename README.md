# Project 86

Helping small business owners stay ahead of what matters.

Project 86 exists to reduce the cognitive load of running a business.

Running a small business means constantly wondering:

- Did I forget something?
- What does this notice mean?
- Is this urgent?
- How much time do I actually have?
- What do I need to do first?

Project 86 helps business owners stay ahead of obligations, understand confusing notices, and know what to do next before small problems become expensive ones.

We are not trying to help business owners remember more.

**We are trying to help them carry less.**

---

# The Problem

Running a small business comes with an endless stream of operational responsibilities:

- License renewals
- Tax notices
- Government filings
- Permit renewals
- Insurance renewals
- Payroll issues
- Health inspections
- Fire inspections
- Sales tax payments
- Countless recurring obligations

Some of these take five minutes.

Others require weeks of preparation, gathering documents, coordinating with accountants, insurance brokers, payroll companies, or government agencies.

The hardest part isn't remembering the deadline.

It's knowing:

- when to start
- what the work actually involves
- whether something is normal or urgent
- who should handle it
- what happens if nothing is done

Most existing tools only remember dates.

Business owners need help understanding everything around those dates.

---

# Our Vision

Project 86 should become the trusted place where business owners:

- stay ahead of important obligations
- understand confusing notices
- reduce unnecessary decisions
- confidently know what to do next

The product should feel like a GPS for running a business.

It doesn't just remind owners where they need to go.

It helps them navigate everything required to get there calmly, confidently, and with enough time.

---

# Why "Project 86"?

In restaurants, "86" means to remove something.

Our mission is to help business owners 86 the uncertainty, stress, and mental burden of running a business.

---

# Version 1 (MVP)

The first version focuses on helping restaurant owners:

- Track recurring operational obligations
- Store related documents
- Receive reminders based on the latest safe start date—not just the due date
- Upload notices and documents for AI-assisted understanding
- Understand what needs attention, why it matters, and what to do next

Restaurants are where we begin.

Small businesses are where we're going.

# Why I'm Building This

I own and operate restaurants.

I've experienced firsthand the mental burden of managing licenses, permits, inspections, tax notices, renewals, and operational obligations while simultaneously trying to run a business.

Some tasks are simple.

Others require significant preparation.

The most stressful moments aren't the deadlines.

They're the moments of uncertainty.

The letter that arrives unexpectedly.

The renewal that requires documents you don't have yet.

The obligation you didn't realize should have been started weeks ago.

I'm building the software I wish I had when I opened my first restaurant.

# Philosophy

Project 86 is built around a few simple ideas.

- Reassure before you alert.
- Reduce unnecessary decisions.
- Hide complexity. Never hide confidence.
- Think ahead so business owners don't have to.
- Help owners understand, not just remember.
