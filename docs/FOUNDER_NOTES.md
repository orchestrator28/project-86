# Founder Notes

## Why Project 86 Exists

Project 86 started because running multiple businesses required us to carry too much operational knowledge in our heads.

Between permits, licenses, renewals, inspections, insurance, passwords, vendors, and recurring obligations, we were constantly asking each other:

> "Did you remember...?"

The mental load was affecting how we ran the business and even spilling into our relationship.

Project 86 exists to become the trusted operational memory for restaurant owners.

The goal isn't to send reminders.

The goal is to reduce cognitive load.

---

## Insight #1

The problem isn't remembering deadlines.

The problem is the cognitive load of carrying operational knowledge in your head.

Project 86 should become the trusted place restaurant owners turn to when they want to know:

> "Am I okay?"

If the answer is "Yes, you're under control," we've done our job.
