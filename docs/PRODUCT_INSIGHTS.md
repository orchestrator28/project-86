# Product Insights

## Insight #001

Restaurant owners don't think in due dates.

They think:

> "Do I have enough time to get this done?"

---

## Insight #002

The most valuable date isn't the due date.

It's the **latest safe start date**.

## Insight #003

Restaurant owners rarely ask for regulations.

They ask for judgment.

Examples:

- Is this normal?
- Is this urgent?
- Can this wait?
- What should I do first?
- Who probably owns this issue?

Project 86 should answer operational questions the way an experienced operator would.
