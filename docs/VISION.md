# Vision

Project 86 exists to reduce the cognitive load of running a business.

Every small business owner carries an invisible mental burden:

- remembering obligations
- interpreting confusing notices
- deciding what matters now
- worrying about what they don't know

Project 86 should become the trusted place where business owners feel safe, understand what they're looking at, and know what to do next.

We are not trying to help people remember more.

We are trying to help them carry less.

## Long-Term Vision

Project 86 can begin with restaurants.

But the real mission is bigger.

It can help any small business owner feel safe, reduce cognitive load, and get help managing the emotional weight of running a business.
