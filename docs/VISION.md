 # Vision

Project 86 exists to reduce the cognitive load of running a business.

Every small business owner carries an invisible mental burden:

- remembering obligations
- interpreting confusing notices
- deciding what matters now
- worrying about what they don't know

Project 86 should become the trusted place where business owners stay ahead of what matters, understand what they're looking at, and know what to do next.

We are not trying to help people remember more.

We are trying to help them carry less.

## Long-Term Vision

Project 86 begins with restaurants because that is where we have deep operational expertise.

But the real mission is bigger.

We want to help any small business owner feel safe, reduce cognitive load, and manage the emotional weight of running a business.

Project 86 should think ahead so business owners don't have to.

## Confidence Layers

Every business owner needs a different level of detail.

Some only need reassurance.

Others want a short explanation.

Others want to inspect the evidence, documents, or regulations behind every recommendation.

Project 86 should reveal information in layers, allowing each person to stop whenever they have enough confidence.

The evidence should always be available.

It should never be required.
